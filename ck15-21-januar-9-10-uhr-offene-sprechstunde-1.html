Art Up Your Tab
Art Up Your Tab shows you enticing and inspiring images from the rich collection of Europeana with every new tab or browser window that you open. The digital platform of Europeana contains approximately 30 million images from over 3,300 archives, libraries and museums. Art Up Your Tab randomly brings you full-screen artworks from a pool of carefully selected images that will pause your busy life for a brief moment and spark your imagination.
http://www.art-tab.eu/
Kunst-Surfer
Kunst-Surfer is a browser add-on project that allows users to repurpose their web-based advertising spaces into an online curatorial platform. The tool will not only serve aesthetic and conceptual purposes, such as questioning and redefining the consumption of (digital) art online, but it will also function as an infrastructure for exposure, promotion and documentation of artworks by Zurich-based off-spaces, independent art spaces, artist-run spaces and platform projects.
https://www.stadt-zuerich.ch/kultur/de/index/foerderung/kultur-labor-zuerich/creative-tech-for-good/kunst-surfer.html
